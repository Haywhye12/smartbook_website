Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about SmartBooks

How does SmartBooks work offline?

SmartBooks downloads all content to your device, allowing students to access textbooks, quizzes, and interactive content without an internet connection. Perfect for areas with limited connectivity.

What subjects are available on SmartBooks?

We offer comprehensive coverage of the Nigerian curriculum for primary and secondary education, including Mathematics, English, Sciences, Social Studies, and more.

How can schools register for bulk access?

Schools can contact us directly for institutional pricing and setup. We provide free training for teachers and dedicated support for educational institutions.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial for new users to explore all features. No credit card required to start your trial.
